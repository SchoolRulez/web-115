Welcome to Food Zoo!

Everything here is edible! Handmade into delightful creatures with a variety of serving methods, anything you want can be made.

Our wonderful chef, a dragon made of warm bread, has been making these fun dishes for several months now. Experienced in the kitchen, the chef decided they wanted to try something new. After receiving all sorts of meal requests from friends and family for food that looks like animals, our chef got right to work and opened up a little restaurant for these wonderful ideas!
