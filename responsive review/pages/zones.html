When you are all ready to go, World of Warcraft has a vast selection of locations to choose from. Usually referred to as zones, there are several in each expansion. The vanilla, classic, or basic set of zones is the most expansive, with forty you could run around in. Each expansion since then has only added more zones to the game and trying to decide where you should go can be a headache. Currently, you can speak to Chromie, a character in the game that can take you back in time to experience any zone during its expansion.

Some of my favorite zones, for story, exploration, or aesthetics include:

Jade Forest

Ardenweald

Suramar
